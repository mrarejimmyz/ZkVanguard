### Acquisition Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **CAC higher than expected** | Medium | High | Diversify channels; improve conversion |
| **Longer sales cycle** | Medium | Medium | Self-serve trial; product-led growth |
| **Competition (Gauntlet)** | Low | High | Emphasize AI + ZK + Gasless differentiators |
| **Market downturn (crypto winter)** | Medium | High | Focus on RWA institutional customers |
| **Churn higher than 1.5%** | Medium | High | Improve onboarding; customer success team |

### Contingency Plans

| Scenario | Trigger | Action |
|----------|---------|--------|
| **CAC > $500** | 2 consecutive months | Pause paid acquisition; double down on content |
| **Conversion < 4%** | Q1 end | Product changes; pricing experiment |
| **Churn > 3%** | 3 consecutive months | Customer success investment; feature prioritization |
